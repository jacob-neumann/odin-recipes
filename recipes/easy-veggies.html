Easy Mixed Vegetables

Health and Tasty!

We all know that eating our vegetables is important, but it isn't always easy to do. This recipe is a simple but delicious way to add a side of mixed vegetables to a meal. By relying on bags of frozen veggies, it is easy to make this dish quickly! This recipe makes 4 servings of mixed vegetables in about 10 minutes of prep (most of which is waiting for the microwave to be done!

Ingredients

  • 1 bag frozen carrots1
  • 1 bag frozen green bans1
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil 2
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)4

Necessary Equipment

  • 1 medium mixing bowl
  • 1 mixing spoon
  • 1 measuring spoon set

Directions

  1. Following the directions on the bags, cook each bag of frozen vegetables separately in the microwave, dumping the contents into the mixing bowl when they are done cooking.
  2. Add the olive oil, salt, and pepper to the veggies, mixing until the veggies are evenly coated
  3. Serve while hot!

Notes

  1. Feel free to use other veggies, like brocoli and cauliflower
  2. You can always add more olive oil! I find that 1 tbsp is just enough to coat everything and help the seasonings adhere
  3. Other seasonings can work too, and can help the veggies pair better with your main course. For instance, adding some Italian seasoning might make it match better with Pasta! I like the simplicity of the salt and pepper though--it highlights the flavors of the veggies.
